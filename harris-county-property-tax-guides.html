How to Protest Property Taxes in Harris County (2026 Guide)

If your property value seems too high in Harris County, Texas, you may be able to file a property tax protest with the Harris Central Appraisal District (HCAD).

Step 1: Review Your Appraisal Notice

  • Check appraised vs prior year value
  • Check for homestead exemption
  • Compare with nearby homes

Step 2: Understand Protest Grounds

Common grounds include market value and unequal appraisal.

Step 3: Gather Evidence

  • Comparable properties
  • Photos or condition issues
  • Repair estimates (if applicable)

Step 4: File Protest

Submit your protest before the deadline listed by HCAD.

Use comparable analysis tools to strengthen your protest evidence.

How to Appeal Your HCAD Assessment

If you disagree with your appraisal from HCAD, you may file a formal protest.

Step 1: Review Assessment

  • Market value estimate
  • Year-over-year changes

Step 2: Choose Protest Basis

Market value or unequal appraisal are common bases.

Step 3: Submit Evidence

  • Comparable homes
  • Supporting documents

Step 4: Review Process

Many cases are resolved during informal review discussions.

Use data-driven comparable analysis to prepare stronger appeal evidence.
